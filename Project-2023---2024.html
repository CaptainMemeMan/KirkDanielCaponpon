Fortnite Horde Mode

Fortnite Horde mode using Unreal Editor for Fortnite (UEFN)

Main Role: Producer and Project Manager

Secondary Role: Level Designer

  • Served as Producer for a Fortnite Horde Mode using Unreal Engine for Fortnite
  • Served as Scrum Leader, conducted weekly meetings managing team sprints and ensuring consistent communication with the client
  • Applied Agile project management practices and led Scrum-based development sprints, incorporating iterative feedback and time-boxed goals
  • Utilized JIRA to manage sprint planning, task tracking, bug documentation, and production timelines
  • Coordinated communication between the team and oversaw development pipelines from pre-production through QA
  • Designed a paper prototype to visualize initial gameplay flow, zone structure, and encounter pacing before moving into engine
  • Created a full level block out in UEFN, establishing key areas such as spawn zones, choke points, and safe rooms to support horde-based gameplay loops
  • Designed the gameplay flow to build tension gradually through progressive enemy difficulty, tight navigation paths, and resource scarcity

Duration: 14 weeks 

Team Size: 4 people

Fortnite Free for All Map Unreal Editor for Fortnite (UEFN)

Main Role: Project Manager

Secondary Role: Level Designer

EndureVR

A Therapy Simulation Virtual Reality Game

Solo Virtual Reality Project

  • Developed a solo prototype project titled EndureVR during the Winter 2023 academic semester, following a direct invitation from a game design professor to explore VR as a therapeutic tool
  • Applied Agile development practices, creating structured weekly sprints and maintaining scope with Kanban-style task management; tracked personal milestones and progress using JIRA
  • Designed a 3D virtual reality simulation focused on phobia exposure therapy, recreating immersive environments and triggering scenarios for fears such as heights (e.g., skyscrapers, bridges, cliffs)
  • Leveraged VR gameplay design principles to emphasize desensitization and gradual exposure, aligning with real-world therapeutic approaches
  • Authored a comprehensive design vision and feature roadmap, balancing game design, user experience, and psychological goals
  • Currently in the prototype phase, with continued refinement and development planned through graduation.

Duration: 15 weeks 

Team Size: 1 people
